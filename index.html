Colonial Dames XVII Century

Looking Glass Chapter
Lansing, Michigan

The Looking Glass Chapter is a local chapter of the National Society Colonial Dames XVII Century, a society dedicated to historic preservation, education, and community service.

Our Society is based on history and heritage. We mark and preserve historic sites to keep history alive and relevant. We take pride in our historic headquarters located in the Dupont Circle Historic District in Washington, D.C.
