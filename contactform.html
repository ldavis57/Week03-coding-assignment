Do you have an ancestor in your family tree who lived in the 17th century and either held an office or owned land?

Someone who fought for our freedom or served in a civil capacity, or aided and supported the patriotic cause in other ways?

Colonial Dames XVII Century has been honoring the legacy of America's early settlers through historical preservation, education, and community service since 1915. With its three-part focus on historic preservation, education, and patriotism, the Colonial Dames XVII Century is all about service. There are innumerable ways to get involved and give back to your community by joining a the Looking Glass chapter (or any chapter!). You will not be alone on your journey, we have members available to assist you as you do family research, and the CDXVIIC library is one of the largest genealogical resources in the world.
